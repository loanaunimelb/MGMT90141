The Story

Parkville Tablet Corporation (PTC) wants to develop a new handheld device for office and university based users. PTC commissioned preliminary product development study to understand its development options and 3 different projects were outlined: one with 5 product features, one with 10 features, and one with 20 features. The financial success of the project depends upon the product features of the handheld device and the chance event concerning the demand for the handheld device.
